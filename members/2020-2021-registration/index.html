Pittsburgh Curling Club Registration

Use the button below to register for the Spring/Summer 2021 session. This session will start on April 19th, 2021 and ends on August 29, 2021. Members can participate in the number of draws that their selected plan provides. Any unused draws expire after August 29, 2021.

The Pittsburgh Curling Club COVID-19 protocols remain in place for the 2021 Spring/Summer session. It is our intent to continue to operate safely under these guidelines until the pandemic abates. The PghCC is committing both the funds and effort necessary to keep the ice and provide the best possible curling experience to our membership during this uncertain time. In return, we ask that you support the PghCC and understand that if club operations are shut down by order of the State of Pennsylvania or the County of Allegheny, we will be unable to provide either full or partial refunds to our membership. By registering as a member, you agree to assume that risk
